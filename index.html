Mesquite & Brush Mitigation

Our signature "Cut Stem + Targeted Herbicide" method delivers long-term control with minimal soil disturbance.

We work year round to restore grazeable acres and improve access by removing invasive or nuisance brush, specializing in mesquite mitigation on North Texas pastures and rangeland. Our focused approach avoids the cost and disturbance of deep grubbing or broad foliar spraying.

Mesquite resprouts from buds at the root collar even after cutting. We cut the trunk flush to slightly below grade and immediately treat the stump and collar with a specialized mixture so the root system does not resprout.

High impact species

We tailor the treatment (cut-stem, basal bark, or selective foliar) to each species.

We can also handle other invasive or nuisance brush species on a case-by-case basis. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Benefits to your land

  1. More usable pasture and better forage access for cattle and horses
  2. Reduced tire and equipment hazards; improved fenceline and road access
  3. Cleaner look without widespread soil disturbance
  4. Clean up your fencelines while keeping the shade cover your livestock needs
  5. Targeted, species-specific treatment to protect desirable trees and grasses

What to expect

  1. On-site walk-through to identify target species and set priorities
  2. Crew removes target brush and treats stumps immediately; minimal rutting/soil disturbance
  3. Photo documentation and simple treatment log
  4. Follow-up inspection at 6-12 months; retreatment of any resprouts in scope
  5. Normal grazing typically can continue; we'll advise on any label-specific restrictions
  6. Expect visible browning/die-back on treated plants over weeks to months; complete root kill is our target
